# Contributing to Awesome Gesture Drawing

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

## What belongs here

This list collects resources that are directly useful for **gesture drawing, figure drawing or life model practice**. That includes :

- Apps and timers
- Pose reference libraries (photo or 3D)
- Books on anatomy, figure drawing, gesture
- Online courses focused on figure or gesture
- YouTube channels that teach figure drawing
- Anatomy references
- Communities of figure drawers
- Hardware (tablets, styluses) used for drawing
- Long form articles or blogs on the practice

## What does not belong here

- Generic art apps (Procreate, Photoshop, Krita) that are not specifically about gesture or figure drawing
- General drawing tutorials that do not focus on gesture or figure
- Tools that are only marginally related (color pickers, font tools, etc.)
- Affiliate or referral links

## How to submit

1. Fork this repo.
2. Add your entry in the relevant section, keeping the alphabetical order.
3. Format : `- [Name](https://url) : short factual description, one to two sentences. Pricing if relevant (Free / Paid / Freemium / Subscription).`
4. Open a pull request with a clear title : `Add NAME to SECTION`.
5. Submit one resource per pull request to make review easier.

## Quality bar

- The resource must be active and accessible (no dead links).
- The description must be factual, not promotional.
- For paid resources, mention the pricing model briefly.
- For your own product, disclose it in the PR description.

## Code of conduct

Be respectful and constructive in discussions. We welcome contributions from artists of all levels.

Thanks for helping make this list better.
